City Latitude vs. Wind Speed


In this graph, each blue dot represents a city for a total of 500 cities. Based on the graph, there is no correlation between the wind speed in a city and its latitude. As a city's latitude increases or decreases, the city's wind speed neither increases nor decreaes. It would appear that a city's wind speed is dependent on other factors.
